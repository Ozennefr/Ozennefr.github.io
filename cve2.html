Exchange semester at chalmers tekniska högskola

jeu. 29 mars 2018 in CV entries
#Chalmers #automotive #engineering #genetic algorithm #optimisation #powertrain #dynamics

I spent the fall 2016 semester at Chalmers in Sweden, where I studied Powertrain engineering, vehicle dynamics, sustainable transportation and biologically inspired optimization methods.

Feedback

This exchange semester was for me a perfect opportunity to test my english proficiency, as well as discover a new culture and new field of studies. Adapting to the new schedule and way of life went well, and allowed me to reflect and sometimes change my old ways. I thouroughly enjoyed visiting sweden and going regularly to hockey matches.
