Engineering degree at UTC

Since autumn 2013, I study engineering at the University of technology of compiègne (UTC). I will graduate in mechanical enginneering (intergrated design speciality) in fall 2018. The flexible nature of UTC's academic path allowed me to follow computer engineering classes.

Volontary work

During my free time I was involved in multiple associations, some of which are presented in their own blogpost.

The one that are not presented are :

  • UTSpace : Realization of a composite rocket head, with the aim of breaking the sound barrier (engine provided by CNES)
  • Find'UTC : a Compiègne city guide for the new students
  • Dataventure : an initiative about sharing knowledge and projects around data sciences (visualisation, machine learning, deep learning, data structures, ...). I did a talk about machine learning and deep learning during one of the afterworks
  • Hackathon : A 24h event were student realize a project. With a team of 4, we participated to the 2017 Hackaton (sponsored by Faurecia, St Gobain and Parrot), and won the fourth prize (jury special prize) for our connected driving wheel concept. The aim of the project was to provide a physical interface between the vehicle and it's occupant, facilitating the switch to autonomous mode via confidence building.
